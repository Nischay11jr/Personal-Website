Who am I?

Nischay is a 19 year old from India and now lives in Cork. Currently, I am a student at University College Cork(UCC) studying BSc(Hons) in Computer Sciences. I was born in New Delhi and completed my high school years from VVDAV Public school.

What do I like?

I have a keen interest in Football. I even played for U-18 Delhi Youth League from Conscient football. I consider the Brazilian Neymar Jr as my idol. I also like to cook sometimes. One of my interesting hobbies is photographing as well. I love going to clubs and socialize with people and get to know about them. I am fond of traveling as well and loves exploring different cultures and traditions about different parts of the world.

What do I think of myself?

I am more practical towards life. I want to do more in my life, never satisfied with myself. I believe that "There is no such thing as motivation. It comes and goes. The only thing that makes you achieve something is your discipline."

What have I achieved yet?

Not a lot of things but enough to keep me motivating to do more. I was my school's one of the MVP players in Football. Also, I got to lead my school team for my last year in high school and we won the zonals that year. I have also been a part of DAV Nationals Football Team. I was also an Enterpreneur in India. I was working with one of my friends in the sneaker world.

What is that one memorable incident that I never forget?

I was in my 10th standard and there were only few weeks left for my final exams. Meanwhile, I was playing a football tournament from Conscient football. I had to appear for the tournament by any means as some recruiters were present at the tournament to recruit for I-League. I still remember the date, it was 10th of September in 2018. My social studies final exam was approaching in the next few days. I did everything I could to convince my Mom to let me go. It was a hard time back then, as there were matches on alternate days, over that the matches were finishing at 9 pm and then I am reaching back home tired, then, I am studying 2 hours straight. At the end of the month, my coach told me that I made it in the I-league summer camp and later the exam results came out to be pretty good as well. I still can't believe how I managed to handle both of them for which it makes a memorable memory.

What is the one thing I am deeply proud of in my life?

The one thing I am proud of myself is engaging myself in different activities, be it sports, being a part of small business or cleaning dishes. I feel really proud of what I have achieved till now as a teenager. I feel that I have not wasted my teenage life in doing what most of the teens do instead gain experience as much as I could. Every activity has taught me some lesson. I believe the people you are surrounded with influence the most. These things has helped me a lot skill wise as well as mentally.
